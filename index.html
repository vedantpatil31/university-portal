About Us

Founded in 1949, Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) is one of India's premier educational institutions with over 700 affiliated colleges and 700,000+ students.

The Department of Technology, established in 1984, focuses on cutting-edge programs in Computer Science, Engineering, and related fields.

Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to innovate, lead, and contribute to society through technological advancement.

Vision

To be a world-renowned university that advances knowledge, fosters innovation, and prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Values

  • Excellence in education and research
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Inclusion and diversity
  • Integrity and ethics

Admissions

Join our vibrant community at Savitribai Phule Pune University. We offer comprehensive admission processes for undergraduate, graduate, and international students.

Undergraduate Programs

Requirements: Maharashtra State CET score, minimum 45% in 12th standard. International students must provide TOEFL/IELTS scores.

Application Period: June-July annually.

Graduate Programs

Bachelor's degree required. GATE/PET scores for M.Tech/PhD programs. Research proposal for doctoral studies.

Application Period: May-June annually.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Government scholarships, merit-based aid, and need-based support available. Over 60% of eligible students receive financial assistance.
